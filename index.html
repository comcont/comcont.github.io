Transistors

The simplest and most essential part of a computer.

Each produces 1 process per second.

One Transistor costs processes.

Microprocessors

These things contain lots and lots of transitors.

Each produces 2 transistors per second.

One Microprocessor costs processes and 100 Transistors.

Macroprocessors

These things contain lots and lots of microprocessors.

Each produces 3 microprocessors per second.

One Macroprocessor costs processes and 1,000 Microprocessors.

Hard Drives

We're finally getting to something useful!

Each produces 4 macroprocessors per second.

One Hard Drive costs processes and 10,000 Macroprocessors.
